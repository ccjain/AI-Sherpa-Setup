# AI Sherpa — Sales Rules

These rules apply to all sales projects. They extend core/CLAUDE.md.

---

## Context Check (Before Every Sales Task)

Before drafting outreach, proposals, or deal materials, confirm:
1. What CRM system is in use (Salesforce, HubSpot, other)?
2. Is there an approved pricing sheet, discount matrix, or proposal template?
3. What is the deal stage and who is the decision-maker?

Do not generate customer-facing materials without knowing the account context.

---

## Always Do

1. Personalize outreach to the specific prospect — no generic blast templates
2. Reference the prospect's known pain points or business context when available
3. State assumptions clearly in any deal model, forecast, or ROI estimate
4. Flag any pricing or discount recommendation with: `⚠ REQUIRES SALES MANAGER APPROVAL`
5. Keep CRM records accurate — flag any data inconsistency you notice rather than silently working around it

---

## Never Do

1. Commit to pricing, discounts, or contract terms without explicit human approval
2. Share competitor intelligence that could expose your company to legal risk
3. Generate fabricated customer testimonials, case studies, or reference quotes
4. Mark a proposal as "sent" or "approved" — that action belongs to the account owner

---

## Outreach Standards

- **Cold outreach:** Keep initial emails under 150 words; one clear call to action
- **Follow-ups:** Reference the prior touchpoint; do not repeat the same message
- **Proposals:** Structure as: problem → solution → evidence → next step → pricing
- **Call prep:** Summarize account history, open items, and stakeholder map before generating talking points

---

## AI Effectiveness Boundaries

Effective: drafting outreach, building talk tracks, summarizing account history, generating proposal structures, pipeline analysis
Not suitable as final authority: pricing decisions, contract terms, forecast commits, deal qualification judgment

---

## Bundled Stack Skills

The globally installed `fullstack-dev-skills` plugin includes a **Salesforce
development** skill that auto-activates when working with Salesforce code or
configuration. No additional install is needed.
