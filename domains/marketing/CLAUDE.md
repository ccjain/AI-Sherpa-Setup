# AI Sherpa — Marketing Rules

These rules apply to all marketing projects. They extend core/CLAUDE.md.

---

## Context Check (Before Every Marketing Task)

Before generating any content or campaign assets, confirm:
1. Is there an approved brand voice guide or style document? If yes, apply it.
2. Does this content reference any product claims, statistics, or competitor comparisons?
3. Is there a legal/compliance review required before publishing?

Do not generate final-publish-ready content without confirming these.

---

## Always Do

1. Cite the source for any statistic, data point, or benchmark you include — never fabricate numbers
2. Label AI-generated drafts clearly in filenames or comments so reviewers know human review is needed
3. Tailor tone to the stated audience — B2B and B2C copy have different conventions; ask if unclear
4. Flag regulatory risk when copy makes health, financial, or legal claims — add: `⚠ COMPLIANCE REVIEW REQUIRED`
5. Check existing brand materials before suggesting new terminology or slogans

---

## Never Do

1. Invent product features, pricing, or availability claims in copy
2. Use competitor names in a way that could constitute disparagement or trademark risk
3. Generate testimonials or reviews — always use real, approved quotes
4. Mark a draft as "final" or "ready to publish" — that decision belongs to the human reviewer

---

## Content Types and Guardrails

- **Ad copy:** Flag any claim that could be read as a guarantee or promise
- **Email campaigns:** Ensure unsubscribe language is present; flag CAN-SPAM / GDPR requirements
- **Social posts:** Flag character/format limits per platform; flag anything that could be misread out of context
- **SEO content:** Do not keyword-stuff — write for readers first, search engines second

---

## AI Effectiveness Boundaries

Effective: drafting, ideation, rephrasing, A/B variant generation, brief summarization, SEO suggestions
Not suitable as final authority: legal compliance review, brand approval, publication decisions, paid media targeting strategy
